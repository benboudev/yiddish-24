How This Extension Works

This Chrome extension enhances your experience on Yiddish 24 by providing the following features:

  • Ad Blocking: Automatically blocks ads on Yiddish 24 for a cleaner and more enjoyable browsing experience.
  • Audio Downloads: Allows you to download audio files directly from Yiddish 24 with a single click.
  • Video Downloads: Enables you to download video files directly from Yiddish 24 with ease.

How It Works

The extension consists of two main scripts: content.js and background.js.

content.js

The content.js script is injected into the Yiddish 24 website and is responsible for adding the download buttons to the audio and video elements on the page. It listens for DOM changes to ensure that newly loaded elements also get the download buttons. When a download button is clicked, it sends a message to the background.js script to handle the download.

background.js

The background.js script listens for messages from content.js and handles the actual downloading of audio and video files. It fetches the files from the server and creates download links to save them to your device. It also sends messages back to content.js to update the UI, such as re-enabling the download buttons after a download is complete.

Who We Are

We are a group of bochurim (students) in yeshiva who love to listen to the inspiring and educational content from Yiddish 24. This extension was created to help us download and listen to the content offline, especially during times when we do not have internet access. We hope this extension will be helpful to others as well.

Instructions

  1. Click the download button below.
  2. Save the zip file to your desired location.
  3. Extract the contents of the zip file to a folder of your choice.
  4. Open Chrome and go to chrome://extensions/.
  5. Enable Developer mode by toggling the switch in the top right corner.
  6. Click the Load unpacked button and select the folder containing the extracted files.
  7. Hit refresh on yiddish24.com.
  8. Enjoy your downloaded content and the extension!
